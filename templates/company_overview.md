## Medium Version (250 words)

MARV Media LLC is a Sacramento, California-based legal marketing company that provides advertising and lead generation services to the legal sector, specifically plaintiff law firms. As a woman and minority-owned business, we bring diverse perspectives to an industry traditionally dominated by large, established players.

Our company operates a hybrid business model combining lead generation with case referrals, targeting the $160 billion U.S. plaintiff contingency-fee market. We specialize in performance-based digital acquisition, utilizing SEO, paid search, paid social, traditional media, digital streaming, and programmatic advertising to generate qualified leads for personal injury, mass tort, and consumer class action cases.

What sets MARV Media apart is our technology-first approach. Our AI-driven operations allow our 3-person team to scale efforts nationally while maintaining personalized service. We leverage advanced case value assessment tools and optimized intake processes to ensure law firm clients receive high-quality, conversion-ready leads.

Our founding team combines over two decades of legal marketing experience, including expertise in multi-channel strategy, legal operations, CRM platforms (Litify, Lead Docket), and case acquisition pipeline optimization. This deep industry knowledge, combined with our lean structure, allows us to deliver better results at lower costs than traditional marketing agencies.

Currently operating with under $100,000 in annual revenue and 3 employees, MARV Media is positioned for significant growth as we expand our client base and service offerings.
